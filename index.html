About Me

Hey! I'm Daksh Kulkarni, a final-year B.Tech CS student, a game & VR developer. My game-dev journey kicked off after playing Uncharted 4. The pacing, story line, and combat sequences made me obsessed with how games create that “feel.”

During my time at SRM University (AP) and a semester at UC Berkeley’s SCET, I focused on game ideas, multiplayer experiences, and XR prototypes. I’ve led and participated in game jams and organized lab events.

My interests: immersive input-based experiences, camera/audio influence, networked transforms, and MR interactions that feel intuitive.
